Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about our HVAC services in Houston.

What HVAC services do you offer in Houston?
We offer comprehensive HVAC services including AC repair, heating repair, new AC and furnace installation, preventative maintenance, ductwork services, thermostat installation, indoor air quality solutions, and AC supply parts. We service all major brands and both residential and commercial properties.
Do you offer emergency or same-day AC repair?
Yes, we provide same-day AC repair services in Houston. If your air conditioner breaks down during business hours, give us a call and we'll get a technician to your home as quickly as possible. We know how urgent AC problems can be in the Texas heat!
How often should I service my HVAC system?
We recommend servicing your HVAC system at least twice a year — once in the spring for your air conditioner before summer, and once in the fall for your heating system before winter. Regular maintenance improves efficiency, extends equipment life, and helps prevent unexpected breakdowns.
Do you sell AC supplies and parts?
Yes! As our name says, we are an AC supply and service company. We provide a wide range of AC supplies, parts, and components including compressors, condensers, evaporator coils, air filters, thermostats, refrigerant, and more for both residential and commercial customers.
What areas of Houston do you serve?
We serve the greater Houston area including Northside, The Heights, Downtown, Midtown, Montrose, Independence Heights, Lindale Park, Woodland Heights, Norhill, Brooksmith, and surrounding communities. If you're in the Houston metro area, give us a call!
How long does a typical AC repair take?
Most AC repairs can be completed within 1-3 hours depending on the complexity. Our service trucks are fully stocked with common parts to ensure fast, efficient service. We'll diagnose the issue, explain the solution, and get your system back up and running — often the same day.
